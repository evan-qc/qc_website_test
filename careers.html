Careers

Want to work with us?

We're a small, hands-on team helping businesses unlock the power of their data through analytics, automation, and AI. If that excites you, we'd love to hear from you.

How to apply

We don't have a formal application form just yet — so for now, the best way to reach us is the simplest one. Send us an email and:

  • Attach your resume.
  • Tell us why you're interested in working for Quantify, and what about us excited or interested you enough to apply.

We read every email and respond to promising candidates within a few business days.
