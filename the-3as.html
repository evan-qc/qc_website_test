About Us

The 3 A's.

Analytics, Automation, and AI — the three technologies we apply to your internal operations wherever a process has become a bottleneck to efficiency or growth.

Analytics

Trusted numbers, seen now instead of at month-end. We build the data foundation — warehousing, modeling, and reporting — that replaces dueling spreadsheets with one shared reality, so leaders can stop firefighting and start running the organization. Learn more →

Automation

Most teams do the same work three times because nothing is connected. We map how the work really happens, redesign it, and automate the repetitive parts — connecting the tools you already own so information flows without double entry. Learn more →

AI

Applied where it earns its keep — not for its own sake. We use AI to handle the work that used to require a person but shouldn't: extracting structure from documents, triaging requests, and surfacing what deserves attention. Learn more →

Why these three, together

As a systems integrator for SMBs, we apply the 3 A's to the internal operations of small and mid-market organizations — typically teams that have outgrown spreadsheets but aren't ready to drop six figures on enterprise software. Our work is for the people accountable for the whole: CEOs, presidents, and public-sector leaders, not a single department.

And we do both halves. The strategy — data modeling, master data, and how information should flow through your business — and the build — warehouses, dashboards, process automation, and system integration. Not advice you're left to implement alone.

The value isn't any one tool. It's what the three A's produce together: trusted numbers, one shared reality, and a deep, written understanding of how your work actually happens — surfacing the workarounds and tribal knowledge that have been quietly holding the company back.
