How we work

01

Find where judgment is repetitive

  • We look for the specific points where someone applies the same simple judgment call over and over: sorting a document, triaging a request, pulling a value out of a form
  • That's where AI adds real leverage - not everywhere, and we'll say so when it isn't the right fit
02

Design the guardrails first

  • We define exactly what the system can decide on its own, and what always routes back to a person
  • Every deployment gets a human checkpoint built in from day one, not added after something goes wrong
03

Build, test, and hand it off

  • We build and test against your real documents and real edge cases, not a demo dataset
  • Your team ends up with a system they understand and can run themselves, not a black box they have to take on faith
